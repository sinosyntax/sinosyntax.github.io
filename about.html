My Profile

Born in San Jose, California, I had the privilege of growing up in a multiethnic learning environment, where I gained invaluable exposure to diverse cultures and values. My early love for music and the humanities blossomed into a deep appreciation for arts education and further shaped my understanding of the world around me.

These early experiences instilled in me a lifetime commitment to education, which I cultivated as a first-generation college student of East Asian Studies, focusing on China. This and other studies in literature and music set the foundation for a twenty-year career exploring various professional roles across Silicon Valley and Asia, including a decade in Shanghai working for architecture and environmental companies in marketing and communications roles.

Today, I have a great interest in instructional design and related areas, as well as a passion for cross-cultural training, adaptive learning, and sustainable design. I am currently enrolled in the Masters of Science program in Learning Technologies and Design at the University of Missouri, Columbia, which I expect to complete in December 2024.
