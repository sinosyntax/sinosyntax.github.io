Select Projects

Fatty's Fresh Cafe

Led development of this boutique cafe on coastal Shanghai, the fulfillment of an aspiration to bring together my love for cooking and spaces that encourage comfort and curiosity. My role was end-to-end implementation spanning project management, business planning, conceptual design, coordination with interior design experts and contractors, menu and recipe formulation, ingredient sourcing, staff training, and cafe operations.

Acrobats of China Promo

This exciting project involved creating a 2-minute promotional video for a nationally-recognized Chinese acrobatic theater troupe. My role focused on orchestrating a multi-camera recording of a live performance, followed by development of a video montage of performance highlights integrated with original graphics, arranged music, and published accolades,resulting in a visual showcase underscoring the troupe's refined artistry and acclaim.

SOM Business Plan

This project came with the responsibility to spearhead the authoring, development, and refinement of critical plan components for the China division of a world-renowned architecture and urban design firm. Collaborating closely with studio leadership and design experts, the project underscores my aptitude for strategic writing, market research, and project coordination.

Tianjin Binhai Master Plan

This project for the Tianjin Binhai New Area Master Plan and its feature in Time+Architecture magazine highlights my diverse contribution. Responsibilities spanned market research, content development, and design collaboration. I undertook market and geographical analyses, authored the magazine feature and several project book sections in close coordination with studio leaders, graphic designers, and publishers.
