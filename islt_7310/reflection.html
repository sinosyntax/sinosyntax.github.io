Gateway to Change

Cultural Connection Nurtures Growth

I believe my diverse educational and professional background offers a unique perspective that influences all my work. My immersion into Asian and Latin cultures early in my life has deepened my appreciation for diverse traditions and values. My love for music and humanities has evolved into a respect for creative learning and design.

Throughout my career, I have had the opportunity to collaborate with global tech and design firms, gaining insights from working with top architecture and environmental firms in China. These experiences have taught me the importance of design that meets user needs and builds strong client relationships.

More

These experiences have also instilled a mindset that drives me to refine my skills, focusing on inclusivity and impactful solutions. I am especially energized by projects that allow me to exercise this mindset across arts education, healthcare, architecture and sustainble urban design.

Today I aim to be a catalyst for change, blending creativity, technical know-how, and a unique lens on the world to help make better sense and better use of a complex and interconnected world.
