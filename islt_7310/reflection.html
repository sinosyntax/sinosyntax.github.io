Gateway to Change

Cultural Connection Nurtures Growth

I believe my diverse educational and professional background offers a unique perspective that influences all my work. My immersion into Asian and Latin cultures early in my life has deepened my appreciation of the traditions and values that motivate educators and learners from all walks of life. My passion for music and the humanities has matured into a profound admiration for creative learning and design excellence.

Throughout my career, I have had the opportunity to work with several global technology and design firms. These experiences have given me a deep respect for the principles and methodologies that drive multi-disciplinary project teams. The privilege of serving top-notch architecture and environmental firms in China has played a critical role in shaping this perspective. It has also positioned me as a strong advocate for design strategies that not only respond to the evolving needs of users but forge meaningful relationships with clients.

This mindset motivates me to continually sharpen my skills and deliver exceptional results. I am dedicated to inclusivity and best practices, and work to deliver transformative solutions that align with the needs of clients and end-users. Collaborative projects that span arts education, healthcare, architecture, urban design, and environmental sustainability particularly energize me.

Overall I aim to be a gateway to change, bringing an astute eye and a blend of creative, technical, and communication skills that empower me think critically and strategically. Dedicated to making a lasting impact, I strive to leverage my unique experience and expertise to respond to a rapidly-evolviing and interconnected world.
