Designing for Change

Cultural Engagement Ignites Ingenuity

Marked by the privilege of calling several countries home, my diverse educational and professional experience gives me a unique viewpoint that I bring to all my work. My early passion for music and humanities has matured into a profound admiration for design excellence and personalized learning. My immersion into Latin and East Asian cultures in my formative years has deepened my appreciation of the rich traditions and values that motivate educators and learners from all walks of life.

Throughout my career, I have had the opportunity to work with several global technology and design firms. These experiences have given me a deep respect for the principles and methodologies that drive communication with multi-disciplinary project teams. The privilege of serving top-notch architecture and environmental firms in China has played a critical role in shaping this perspective. It has also positioned me as a strong advocate for design strategies that not only respond to the evolving needs of users but forge meaningful relationships with clients.

This mindset holds immense significance to me as it is not mere aspiration, but a driving force to make a significant difference in people's lives. Guided by a vision for inclusivity and best practices, I am determined to continually sharpen my skills and facilitate transformative solutions that cater to the varied needs of clients and end-users. Collaborative projects that span arts education, healthcare, architecture, urban design, and environmental sustainability particularly ignite my motivation.

To these endeavors, I bring an astute eye and a blend of creative, technical, and communication skills that empower me to approach challenges with ingenuity and poise. With a dedication to ongoing growth and development, I aim to consistently deliver tangible results that leave a lasting, positive impact on individuals and communities alike.
